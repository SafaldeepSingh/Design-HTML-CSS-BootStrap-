Our Services

Real Estate Services

Our expertise includes more than assisting you in a real estate transaction. We have a history of expertise on both the analytical and financial sides of a real estate transaction. Before any decisive steps are taken in a project, we work with you to understand where the financial risks and opportunities lie. We have developed properties and various types of projects. We pride ourselves on providing individual attention, a thorough market analysis, and the most up-to-date invaluable insider’s perspective on the state’s various regions and submarkets

Planning & Due Diligence

We have made it our priority to become experts at performing due diligence functions and assessing the underlying value of a given real estate transaction. Planning is a single decisive factor determining the success or failure of a given project or venture. While our services are extremely diverse, our goals remain constant – to assess value and to determine the most advantageous time to realize the value.

Our strategic planning provides a wide range of business planning for clients, lender and investors. We are experienced in designing solutions to all different types of projects, including structuring entities and transactions, minimizing tax costs and customized financing. Our transactional experience also includes acquisitions and development, equity investment and traditional financing, construction, leasing, workouts and debt restructuring, land use and zoning matters.

Development

We provide entrepreneurial, practical solutions to challenges of development and bring a high degree of responsiveness and creativity to every project, as well as the ability to complete projects in a timely and cost-effective fashion.

The arrays of services available from ATLANTIC in support of a development program are comprehensive and include:

-Market Feasibility Analyses
-Site Selection & Entitlement
-Planning & Management
-Development Management Consulting
-Turnkey Development Management Services
-Owner Representation Services
-Asset Valuation & Disposition

Atlantic will assist clients to complete projects and transactions in a timely manner, within budget, and with minimal exposure to inherent risks of creating value for our clients by utilizing innovative applications of engineering assessments, environmental studies, and well proven due diligence techniques.
